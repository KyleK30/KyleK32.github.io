Sports Jeopardy!!

This is an online version of Sports Jeopardy!! It is completely free for anyone to play. The different sports include: Basketball, Baseball, Cricket, Football, Hockey, and Soccer.
There are three categories about the different sports each with four different questions. The questions may range from who won the championship that year, or who the MVP was. Have fun and good luck!
